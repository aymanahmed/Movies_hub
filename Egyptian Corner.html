# Evolution trends of Egyptian movies industry over the years analysis.
-In 1932, the first speaking movie was produced featuring Youssef wahby and Amina Rezq.
- Studio Masr was established in 1935, and it was the center of Egyptian movies industry till the Second World War.
- By The ends of the Second World War in 1945 the number of movies produced a year start to increase.
- In the 60s the Egyptian movies industry was nationalized by Abd El Nasser the Egyptian president back then, which causes a noticeable decrease in the numbers of movies and the cinemas from 354 to 255 cinemas.
- 1971 was the year when the nationalization of the Egyptian movies industry ended but the rate of producing movies remained low.
- 80s was the start of new cinema era "the realism", and the most important actors of this era are Adel Emam, Ahmed Zaki and Nour El Shreef.
- 1984 was the start of increasing the popularity of "Contracting movies" this movies have low budget movies with poor content which were produced then exported to the Khaleej countries, and this type of movies increase the number of movies to its peak over the years.
-In 1988, the ends of Khaleej war and this is when the order on contracting movies declined and that bring the rate back down.
-1997, start of comedy era by the production of one of the most successful comedy movies "Esmaailya Rayh Gai", which increase the number of comedy movies.
-the years 2006, 2007 and 2008 are show a trend of increasing movies till 2009 when rate of movies start to decrease again especially in 2011 due to the political events.
